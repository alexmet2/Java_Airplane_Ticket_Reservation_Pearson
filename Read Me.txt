For this project to work properly, you have to open it from Apache NetBeans IDE 

To open it you have to:

1) Click on the tab 'File' on top of the screen
2) Hover on the tab 'Import File'
3) Click on 'From ZIP'
4) Select the correct directory of the file/project you want to open
5) Select your preffered path for the zip file to save
6) Click Import
